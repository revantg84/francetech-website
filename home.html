Welcome to France & Technology

Discover the dynamic fusion of French heritage and cutting-edge innovation. Our website is dedicated to exploring how France has been a pioneer in technology—from the early days of video game development to today’s revolutionary startups and futuristic innovations.

What You'll Find Here

  • History: Learn about the evolution of video games in France, from its humble beginnings to its status as a global leader in gaming design.
  • Innovation: Dive into France’s trailblazing technological breakthroughs, including iconic projects like Minitel, TGV, and advancements in AI and aerospace.
  • Startup Culture: Explore how French IT startups like BlaBlaCar, Doctolib, and OVHcloud are reshaping the digital economy and driving the nation's economic growth.
  • The Future: Get insights into emerging trends and future technologies that promise to transform the way we live and work.
  • References & Contributions: Access curated resources and meet the experts and contributors who share their knowledge and passion for French technology.

Join us on this journey through France’s remarkable contributions to technology, and see how innovation and tradition blend to create a legacy of excellence.
